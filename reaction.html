Reaction Test

This test is to test your raw reaction time. It is similar to the aim trainer where you have to react, except you don't have to move your cursor.

The way your score is calculated is you will be asked to click once the screen turns green, this process repeats 5 times. At the end it will combine all of your time and then divide it by 5, giving it your average raw reaction time.
